Tongkat Ali
Testosterone Support

Also called Eurycoma Longifolia, this Southeast Asian root extract has been studied for its ability to reduce sex hormone-binding globulin (SHBG), which frees up more active testosterone in the bloodstream. Clinical evidence indicates it may improve male fertility markers and athletic performance in older men. It is one of the most well-researched natural testosterone modulators available.

Tribulus Terrestris
Libido Enhancement

A Mediterranean plant long used in traditional medicine for male vitality, Tribulus contains steroidal saponins that research suggests may increase luteinizing hormone (LH) secretion, indirectly signaling the testes to produce more testosterone. Studies show it may also support sperm motility and overall reproductive health in men with suboptimal baseline levels.

Horny Goat Weed (Epimedium)
Blood Flow Support

Icariin, the active compound in Epimedium, functions as a natural PDE5 inhibitor, meaning it works along the same biological pathway as certain prescription ED medications but through a milder, plant-derived mechanism. Research suggests it supports nitric oxide pathways and enhances penile blood flow. It is one of the key reasons endopeak male enhancement users report improved physical performance.

Ashwagandha Extract
Cortisol Control

Ashwagandha is one of the most clinically validated adaptogenic herbs available. Multiple randomized trials have shown it significantly reduces serum cortisol, and because cortisol directly suppresses testosterone biosynthesis, lowering cortisol creates favorable conditions for natural hormone production. It also supports thyroid function and reduces perceived stress, which has downstream benefits for libido and energy.

Saw Palmetto
DHT Regulation

Saw Palmetto berry extract inhibits the enzyme 5-alpha reductase, which converts testosterone into dihydrotestosterone (DHT). By managing DHT conversion, more usable testosterone remains available for energy, libido, and muscle function. Clinical evidence indicates it also supports prostate health, making it a dual-purpose addition to a male health formula.

L-Arginine HCl
Nitric Oxide Precursor

L-Arginine is a semi-essential amino acid that serves as the direct precursor to nitric oxide synthesis in the body. Increased nitric oxide output relaxes and widens blood vessels, improving circulation throughout the body including to the genitourinary system. Studies show it is most effective when combined with other vasodilatory compounds, as is the case in this formula.

Hawthorn Berry
Cardiovascular Support

Hawthorn berry is valued in cardiovascular herbalism for its ability to strengthen arterial walls and improve coronary blood flow. Research suggests it reduces peripheral vascular resistance, complementing the nitric oxide effects of L-Arginine by improving the structural health of blood vessels. It also carries mild antioxidant properties that protect vascular tissue from oxidative stress.

Chrysin
Aromatase Inhibition

Chrysin is a bioflavonoid found in passionflower and honey that acts as a mild natural aromatase inhibitor. Aromatase is the enzyme responsible for converting testosterone into estrogen, and managing this conversion helps maintain a healthier testosterone-to-estrogen ratio. While bioavailability is a known challenge, its inclusion alongside absorption-supportive ingredients addresses this concern to a degree.
